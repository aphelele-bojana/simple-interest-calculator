# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback
- Inclusive behavior

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces and interactions.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in warnings or bans.

## Enforcement Guidelines
- Correction
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
